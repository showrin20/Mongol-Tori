Rover in The Lands of BICC

During the pandemic, meetups and demonstrations were out of the question. Hence, when the time came to demonstrate the rover at a national expo BRACU Mongol-Tori jumped upon the opportunity. International conference on 4th Revolution and Beyond (IC4IR) 2021 was held on 10-11th December 2021. This event, organised by UGC held on the premises of Bangabandhu International Conference Center (BICC), was a golden opportunity for the companies and students to showcase their ideas at the conference. The event was held to mark the beginning of the 4th Industrial revolution, where industrial companies showcased their new products. The event had presentations from many aspiring undergraduate students with their project ideas and dreams. ICT State Minister Zunaid Ahmed Palak, Education Minister Dr. Dipu Moni, other esteemed judges, and government officials attended the event. The conference had automated drones, racing cars, automated drones, mars rovers, and autonomous underwater vehicles, with many other projects made by the students. BRACU Mongol-Tori, along with BRACU Duburi, BRAC Onnesha, and ROBU represented BRAC University. Mars rover, with all its shining metal and lights, greeted the ICT Minister. With the urgent need to prepare for the upcoming 4th Industrial revolution, the conference also had a small seminar. The Seminar focused on the impacts and how students can prepare themselves. It is a golden opportunity to show the rover and its uses to mass audiences. Mongol-Tori is grateful to BRAC University for giving us the opportunity.
